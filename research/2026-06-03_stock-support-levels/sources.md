## [研究] 学术期刊 / 央行研究（本主题最高权威）

- [研究] Osler, "Support for Resistance: Technical Analysis and Intraday Exchange Rates"（FRBNY Economic Policy Review 6:2, 53–68）— https://www.newyorkfed.org/medialibrary/media/research/epr/00v06n2/0007osle.pdf — 2000 — 直接检验已发布支撑/阻力位，对日内汇率趋势中断有显著（但因币种/机构而异）预测力（PDF 本轮 403，结论以 RePEc 摘要核实）。
- [研究] Osler, "Currency Orders and Exchange Rate Dynamics: An Explanation for the Predictive Success of Technical Analysis"（Journal of Finance 58:5, 1791–1819）— https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1111/0022-1082.00592 — 2003 — 止盈单聚集整数位→解释反转；止损单聚集整数位外→解释突破后加速。机制层面最硬证据。
- [研究] Osler, FRBNY Staff Report No. 125 — "Currency Orders and Exchange-Rate Dynamics" — https://www.newyorkfed.org/medialibrary/media/research/staff_reports/sr125.pdf — 2000 — 约 10% 止损/止盈单挂在以"00"结尾整数位、其余以"0"结尾价位平均约 3% 的数据来源（一手 PDF 本轮 403，比例依期刊版与权威转引，未独立核实原始 PDF）。
- [研究] Osler, "Stop-loss orders and price cascades in currency markets"（JIMF 24:2, 219–241）— https://ideas.repec.org/a/eee/jimfin/v24y2005i2p219-241.html — 2005 — 止损触发引发正反馈/价格瀑布，解释穿越聚集位后的快速移动与肥尾。
- [研究] Brock, Lakonishok &amp; LeBaron, "Simple Technical Trading Rules and the Stochastic Properties of Stock Returns"（Journal of Finance 47:5, 1731–1764）— https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1540-6261.1992.tb04681.x — 1992 — 道指 1897–1986，均线+交易区间突破收益不符随机游走等四个零模型（经典正面证据，未扣交易成本）。
- [研究] Lo, Mamaysky &amp; Wang, "Foundations of Technical Analysis"（NBER WP 7613 / Journal of Finance 55:4, 1705–1765）— https://www.nber.org/papers/w7613 — 2000 — 核回归自动识别头肩/双底等形态，部分形态对美股有"增量信息、可能有实用价值"（措辞克制）。
- [研究] Park &amp; Irwin, "What Do We Know About the Profitability of Technical Analysis?"（Journal of Economic Surveys 21:4, 786–826）— https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1111/j.1467-6419.2007.00519.x — 2007 — 95 项现代研究 56 正/20 负/19 混；"至少到 1990 年代初"持续获利但带强烈方法论保留（56/20/19 已直接核实）。
- [研究] Sullivan, Timmermann &amp; White, "Data-Snooping, Technical Trading Rule Performance, and the Bootstrap"（Journal of Finance 54:5, 1647–1691）— https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/0022-1082.00163 — 1999 — White's Reality Check 校正数据挖掘后，BLLL 最优规则约十年样本外失效：本主题最关键反证。
- [研究] Fama, "Efficient Capital Markets: A Review of Theory and Empirical Work"（Journal of Finance 25:2, 383–417）— https://ideas.repec.org/a/bla/jfinan/v25y1970i2p383-417.html — 1970 — 弱式 EMH：历史价量已被 price-in，技术分析不能持续超额（反方理论总纲）。
- [研究] Tversky &amp; Kahneman, "Judgment under Uncertainty: Heuristics and Biases"（Science 185:4157, 1124–1131）— https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/science.185.4157.1124 — 1974 — 锚定—调整启发法，整数/心理价位叙事的理论根（讲认知偏差，非交易获利证据）。
- [研究] Mitchell &amp; Izan, "Clustering and psychological barriers in exchange rates"（JIFMIM 16:4, 318–344）— https://ideas.repec.org/a/eee/intfin/v16y2006i4p318-344.html — 2006 — 独立证实外汇整数位聚集/心理屏障（非 Osler 单一来源）。
- [研究] "Automatic identification and evaluation of Fibonacci retracements: Empirical evidence from three equity markets"（Expert Systems with Applications）— https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0957417421012495 — 2021 — 三市场逻辑回归：斐波那契区与随机价位反弹概率统计不可区分（直接证伪其非随机性；全文 403，结论以摘要核实）。
- [研究] "Psychological barriers in European stock markets: Where are they?"（Journal of Banking &amp; Finance）— https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S1044028308000549 — 2008 — 整数/心理屏障在欧洲股市的实证与锚定根因（全文 403，结论以摘要核实）。
